Latvian bacon buns recipe

Description

Latvian bacon buns, known as "Pīrāgi" or "Speķa rauši," are savory pastries filled with bacon and onions. They are a popular and delicious snack or appetizer in Latvia. Latvian bacon buns are a beloved treat for their flaky pastry and savory bacon-onion filling. They are perfect for a snack, party, or as a side dish. Here's a recipe to make Latvian bacon buns:

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup bacon, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped and sautéed until translucent
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

Prepare the Dough:

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the sour cream, softened butter, and egg.
  4. Pour the yeast mixture into the sour cream mixture and stir to combine.
  5. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the flour mixture and knead until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  6. Cover the dough with a clean cloth and let it rise for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

Prepare the Filling:

  1. Cook the bacon until it's crispy and finely chop it. Also, sauté the finely chopped onion until it's translucent.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked bacon, sautéed onions, and season with salt and black pepper to taste. Mix well.

Assemble the Bacon Buns:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Roll out the risen dough on a floured surface until it's about 1/4 inch thick.
  3. Cut out circles from the dough using a round cutter or a glass.
  4. Place a small spoonful of the bacon and onion filling in the center of each dough circle.
  5. Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape, then pinch the edges to seal the bun.

Bake the Bacon Buns:

  1. Place the assembled bacon buns on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.

Serve:

  1. Let the bacon buns cool slightly, then serve them as a delightful snack or appetizer. They can be enjoyed warm or at room temperature.
